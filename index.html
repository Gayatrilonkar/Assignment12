Full Stack Developer!!!

A full-stack developer helps build and maintain both the front-end and the back-end of a website. Learn about full-stack developer skills, salary, and how you can become one.

A full stack developer is a developer or engineer who can tackle projects that involve databases, APIs, build user-facing websites, or even work with clients during the planning phase of projects. Full stack developer skills: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and one or more back end languages.
